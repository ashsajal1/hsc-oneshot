# Math Formula Examples

## Inline Math

The quadratic formula is $x = \frac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a}$

## Block Math

$$
E = mc^2
$$

## Complex Formulas

### Integral

$$
\int_{-\infty}^{\infty} e^{-x^2} dx = \sqrt{\pi}
$$

### Summation

$$
\sum_{n=1}^{\infty} \frac{1}{n^2} = \frac{\pi^2}{6}
$$

### Matrix

$$
\begin{pmatrix}
a & b \\
c & d
\end{pmatrix}
$$

### Piecewise Function

$$
f(x) = \begin{cases}
x^2 & \text{if } x \geq 0 \\
-x & \text{if } x < 0
\end{cases}
$$
